About Me

Hi! My name is Grant Dumanian and I'm a junior at Dartmouth College in Hanover, New Hampshire, although I am originally from San Francisco. I am currently studying Computer Science and Spanish, with a particular interest in creating user-friendly front end frameworks that leverage powerful APIs. Outside of the classroom, I am an avid rock climber, hiker, and snowboarder. Whenever possible, I spend my free time in the mountains.

Most of my professional experience has been spent working as a Product Manager at different tech startups. During the summer of 2018, a coworker and I traveled to Nepal on behalf of a local non-profit and field tested 'Looma,' an educational software designed for low income classrooms. During the summer of 2019, I was a PM intern at Grove Collaborative, an eco-friendly home goods company, where I transitioned the product team from a front-end to a full stack testing software. Most recently, I was a product manager at Mana.ai, where I established a scalable program for the hiring of freelance coaches and hired and trained two personal coaches to guide users.
